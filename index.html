Who We Are

Where ethics and fashion finally meet.

BEGOOD Sustainable Clothing Sourcing Limited (BGSCSL) is a registered social business based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We source and deliver ethically made garments built on organic materials, fair labour practices, and zero-waste principles — for buyers around the world who care about where their clothes come from.

"We aim to create a world where fashion is a force for good — nurturing the environment and empowering every individual in the supply chain."

Environmental Commitment

Responsible at
every step.

We integrate environmental responsibility into everything — from the seed in the ground to the garment on your shelf.

Organic Inputs Only

All raw materials are sourced without harmful pesticides or synthetic fertilizers. Clean from field to fabric — no shortcuts.

Zero Waste Production

Energy-efficient facilities, minimised water consumption, and full material recycling throughout every stage of the production cycle.

Carbon Offset Programs

We invest in reforestation, renewable energy projects, and sustainable agriculture to offset our footprint and work toward a net positive environmental impact.

Ethical Supply Chain

Every partner in our supply chain meets verified fair labour standards — transparent working conditions, fair wages, and documented accountability from source to delivery.

Our goal: zero carbon by 2030.

Every order you place contributes to this journey.

Community & Social Impact

People are at
the heart of
what we do.

A thriving fashion industry cannot be built on exploitation. BEGOOD is committed to actively engaging with — and giving back to — the communities that make our work possible.

01

Empowering Local Artisans

Fair wages and real growth opportunities for skilled Bangladeshi craftspeople. We preserve cultural heritage through every stitch while building economic security for workers and their families.

02

Education & Awareness

Workshops and community outreach on sustainable fashion choices — building a consumer base that understands the impact of what they wear and chooses responsibly.

03

Community Development

Active support for poverty alleviation, gender equality, and environmental conservation — partnering with local organisations to create lasting, systemic change beyond our business operations.
